This web app was designed by amazing students at the University of Pretoria in the year 2014

It is designed as one of the front-end interfaces to the Quantum Facial Recognition Secuirty System (QFRSS). Its' main function is to provide the user with the ability to check a face against the community database and view returned results filtered by date and time.
